About Me

Hi! My name is Emily and I have two kids, Caroline and Austin, who both love a good playground. I love a good playground too, but while my kids are checking to see if the playground has monkey bars and spinners, I'm checking out the parking lot and bathroom situation. I decided to create this website as a go-to source for parents who are looking for a playground in Louisville that meets their kids' needs while also providing everything the parents need to feel safe and comfortable.

Brown Park

1000 Browns Lane, Louisville, KY 40207

What the kids say:

  • Fun slides
  • Spinner
  • Lots of things to climb
  • No zip line
  • No swings
  • No water feature

What I say:

  • Good visual of entire playground
  • Covered pavillion nearby
  • Clean bathrooms
  • No shade on playground
  • Parking lot can be hard to navigate

Eastover Park

What I say:

Big Rock Park

2199 Park Boundary Rd, Louisville, KY 40205

  • Fake rocks to climb

  • No zip line
  • No big slides

What I say:

  • Good visual of entire playground
  • Parking lot is close to playground

Big Rock! How can you not love playing around in Beargrass Creek?
